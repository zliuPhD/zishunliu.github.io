Research

My research interests encompass the fields of control theory, stochastic systems, probability, optimization, and machine learning. My current research is focused on but not limited to: the safety, reachability and of stochastic control systems, as well as their application in autonomy.

  • Safety of Stochastic Systems. Design computationally tractable controllers that provide formal, high-probability (e.g., 99.99%) safety guarantees for nonlinear stochastic systems.
  • Reachability Analysis under Uncertainties. For systems under uncertainties, develop computationally efficient methods to approximate their reachable sets, and leverage them in safe controller synthesis.
  • Application of Safe Control in Autonomy Synthesize real-time implementable control policies that provide provable safety guarantees for autonomy dynamical systems under uncertainties.
